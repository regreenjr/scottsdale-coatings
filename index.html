The Process

Bare concrete to
showroom floor.

No shortcuts, no peeling. Every floor runs the same five steps that make a coating last for decades — not seasons.

01

Repair

Fill cracks, chips and pitting for a sound, even slab to build on.

02

Diamond Grind

Open the concrete's pores so the coating bonds permanently — the step cheap crews skip.

03

Base Coat

A fast-curing polyurea base goes down as the foundation of the system.

04

Broadcast Flake

Hand-thrown vinyl flake to full refusal in your chosen color and chip size.

05

Top Coat

UV-stable polyaspartic locks it in with a tough, glossy, easy-clean finish.
